Jalal-Abad region

Jalalabad is a city in southern Kyrgyzstan, serving as the administrative center of the Jalal-Abad Region.
Situated along the Kurgan-Tyube River, it has a rich history, including its time as part of the Great Chuy Valley Khanate.
The city's economy is based on agriculture, textile industries, and other sectors. Jalalabad is a cultural hub,
featuring numerous monuments and museums that showcase Kyrgyz and Central Asian
history. Tourism is significant, with the city surrounded by picturesque natural landscapes,
and cultural events and festivals attracting visitors.
